TANK BEER

Březňák 11 light lager:

In the picturesque Pivovar Velké Březno, Březňák beer began to be brewed as early as 1753, and to this day it is like an "old school", honest, high-quality, brewed using traditional technological procedures and with love for honest craftsmanship. The year 1906 was a turning point in the history of the Březňák brand. It was then that a trademark was registered with the Chamber of Commerce in Liberec, on which the face of the satisfied beer connoisseur and head of the station in Velké Březn, Victor Cibich, appeared for the first time. It is an inherent symbol of the brand and its quality to this day.
